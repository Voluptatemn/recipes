Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made of layered sheets of pasta interspersed with a variety of savory fillings. It's typically baked in an oven, creating a hearty, rich, and flavorful meal with a golden, bubbling top layer of melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 medium carrot (chopped)
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 can (28 oz / 800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1/2 cup beef or chicken broth (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (to balance acidity)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (divided)
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese (divided)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 9-12 lasagna pasta sheets (boil if needed, or use oven-ready)
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese (for topping)
  • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)

Steps

Meat Sauce

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Cook for 5-7 minutes until softened and fragrant.
  2. Cook the Meat: Add the ground beef (or beef and pork mix) to the pan. Cook until browned, breaking it up into small pieces. Drain any excess fat, if necessary.
  3. Build the Sauce: Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine, and broth. Stir well to combine.
  4. Season It: Add the sugar, basil, oregano, bay leaves, salt, and black pepper. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Simmer the Sauce: Bring the sauce to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Let it simmer uncovered for 25-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened. Remove the bay leaves before using.

Prepare the Cheese Mixture

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella, 3/4 cup of Parmesan cheese, the egg, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix until fully combined.

Assemble the Lasagna

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Layer 1 (Base Layer): Spread a thin layer of meat sauce (about 1/2 cup) at the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish to prevent the pasta from sticking.
  3. Add the Pasta: Place 3-4 lasagna sheets on top of the sauce, covering the base
  4. Add the Cheese: Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the pasta.
  5. Add the Meat Sauce: Spoon 1/3 of the meat sauce on top.
  6. Repeat the Layers: Repeat the layers (pasta, ricotta cheese mixture, meat sauce) 2-3 times, depending on the depth of your dish.
  7. Top It Off: For the final layer, add the last of the meat sauce, pour the béchamel sauce (if using) on top, and sprinkle 1/2 cup mozzarella and 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese for a golden, bubbly finish.

Bake the Lasagna

  1. Cover with Foil: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (to prevent the top from burning) and bake for 25 minutes.
  2. Remove Foil: Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until the top is bubbly, golden brown, and slightly crispy.
  3. Rest the Lasagna: Remove from the oven and let it cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing. This resting period helps the layers set, making it easier to serve.

Enjoy!

  1. Slice the Lasagna: Use a sharp knife to cut square portions of lasagna.
  2. Garnish: Garnish with fresh basil leaves and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top.
  3. Serve It Hot: Serve hot with garlic bread or a side salad.
